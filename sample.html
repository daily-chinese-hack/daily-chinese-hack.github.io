Scene 1: Gift Wrapping

Sentence 1

English: Please pick a color of wrapping paper.

Chinese: 请选择一种包装纸的颜色。(Qǐng xuǎnzé yī zhǒng bāozhuāngzhǐ de yánsè.)

Quick Tip: The structure directly follows the English order: "请" (please) + "选择" (pick) + "一种" (a color of) + "包装纸" (wrapping paper) + "的" (of) + "颜色" (color).

Chinglish: Please pick one kind wrapping paper color.

Vocabularies: 请 (qǐng) - please, 选择 (xuǎnzé) - pick, 一种 (yī zhǒng) - a kind of, 包装纸 (bāozhuāngzhǐ) - wrapping paper, 颜色 (yánsè) - color

Sentence 2

English: Would you like it wrapped?

Chinese: 你想要把它包装起来吗？(Nǐ xiǎng yào bǎ tā bāozhuāng qǐlái ma?)

Quick Tip: The "把" (bǎ) structure is used to indicate handling an object: "把 + object + verb (包装)". "起来" indicates the action's completion.

Chinglish: You want take it wrap up?

Vocabularies: 你 (nǐ) - you, 想要 (xiǎng yào) - want, 把 (bǎ) - (particle for handling object), 它 (tā) - it, 包装 (bāozhuāng) - wrap, 起来 (qǐlái) - up

Sentence 3

English: I'll iron your clothes before wrapping them.

Chinese: 我会在包装前熨烫你的衣服。(Wǒ huì zài bāozhuāng qián yùntàng nǐde yīfú.)

Quick Tip: Time is expressed with "在... 前" (before...). The sentence order is: Subject (我) + will (会) + before (在... 前) + action (熨烫) + object (你的衣服).

Chinglish: I will at wrap before iron your clothes.

Vocabularies: 我 (wǒ) - I, 会 (huì) - will, 在... 前 (zài...qián) - before, 包装 (bāozhuāng) - wrapping, 熨烫 (yùntàng) - iron, 你的 (nǐde) - your, 衣服 (yīfú) - clothes

Sentence 4

English: Can we have them wrapped together?

Chinese: 我们可以把它们一起包装吗？(Wǒmen kěyǐ bǎ tāmen yīqǐ bāozhuāng ma?)

Quick Tip: Again, uses the "把" structure for handling multiple objects "它们" (them). "一起" (together) is placed before the verb.

Chinglish: We can take them together wrap?

Vocabularies: 我们 (wǒmen) - we, 可以 (kěyǐ) - can, 把 (bǎ) - (particle for handling object), 它们 (tāmen) - them, 一起 (yīqǐ) - together, 包装 (bāozhuāng) - wrap

Sentence 5

English: Do you need to pick a card?

Chinese: 你需要选一张卡片吗？(Nǐ xūyào xuǎn yī zhāng kǎpiàn ma?)

Quick Tip: The verb "选" (pick) directly follows the modal verb "需要" (need). "一张" is the measure word for flat objects like cards.

Chinglish: You need pick one card?

Vocabularies: 你 (nǐ) - you, 需要 (xūyào) - need, 选 (xuǎn) - pick, 一张 (yī zhāng) - one (for flat objects), 卡片 (kǎpiàn) - card
